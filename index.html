Hi, I'm Jared.

I'm a reporter at Tax Notes, where I cover federal tax policy and the people who shape it. Most of my work focuses on the White House, Treasury Department and Congress, following the decisions, debates and personalities behind major tax legislation and regulation.

Before joining Tax Notes, I covered government, politics and public policy in a variety of reporting roles. I've written breaking news, enterprise stories and profiles, and have interviewed lawmakers, government officials and industry leaders.
